Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sept. 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how HitTips (“the App,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Subscription details (via Apple/RevenueCat). We do not collect credit card numbers.
  • Usage Data: Device type, app interactions, crash logs.
  • User Inputs: Tip amounts and roast prompts (processed to generate responses).
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the App’s features.
  • Process subscriptions and entitlements.
  • Deliver AI-generated roasts and entertainment features.
  • Show ads (for free users).
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Firebase (analytics, crash reporting)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • OpenAI (AI text generation)

We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the App and meet legal obligations. Tip history is stored locally on your device unless deleted.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict use of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps (encryption, limited access) to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.
